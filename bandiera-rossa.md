## Bandiera Rossa

Red flag will be triumphant

Red flag will triumph.

Red flag will be triumphant  
Red flag will be triumphant  
Red flag will be triumphant  
Only socialism is true freedom.

The borders are red flags  
Oh socialists, to the rescue  
Red flag will triumph.

Red flag will be triumphant  
Red flag will be triumphant  
Red flag will be triumphant  
Only in socialism is there peace and freedom.

Red flag will triumph.

Red flag will be triumphant  
Red flag will be triumphant  
Red flag will be triumphant
